How ClutterGo Works

1. Quick chat & photos

We start with a short call or text and a few photos of your space so we can see what’s going on and what a “win” looks like for you.

2. Calm, simple plan

Together we choose where to start, set a pace that feels comfortable, and decide what’s keep, donate, sell or remove.

3. Sort, clear & breathe

We work alongside you to declutter. When you’re ready, our rubbish partner team removes the unwanted items so you’re left with clean space.

4. Support after the session

Need help deciding what to sell or donate? We can give simple resale tips and ideas for keeping your space under control.
